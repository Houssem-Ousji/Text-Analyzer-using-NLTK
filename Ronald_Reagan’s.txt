The rangers looked up and saw the enemy soldiers at the edge of the cliffs shooting down
at them with machine guns and throwing grenades, and the American rangers began to climb.
They shot rope ladders over the face of these cliffs and began to pull themselves up.
When one ranger fell, another would take his place. When one rope was cut,
a ranger would grab another and begin his climb again. 
They climbed, shot back, and held their footing.
Soon, one by one, the rangers pulled themselves over the top, and in seizing the firm land
at the top of these cliffs they began to seize back the continent of Europe… (to veterans)
You all knew that some things are worth dying for. One’s country is worth dying for, and Democracy
is worth dying for because it’s the most deeply honorable form of government ever devised by man
